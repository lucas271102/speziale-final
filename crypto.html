CRYPTO.

Bitcoin

Bitcoin is a digital currency that everyone can acquire in every crypto exchange. It cannot be controlled by an authority. Bitcoin consists in a cryptographic key that can be administrated in a virtual wallet.

Ethereum

Ethereum is a digital platform that adopts the blockchain technology. Ether is its native cryptocurrency. It was created By Vitalik Buterin with one goal: To make an instrument for decentralized and colaborative apps.

It works as a shared digital book where all the transactions can be administrated

Litecoin

It was designed to be used in small transactions and to be more efficient. Transactions with litecoin are faster than bitcoin or ether, but the litecoin amount is much bigger , so the profit you can get is not high
